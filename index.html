Work Hard. Play Hard.

HackSmith is Smith's 4th annual hackathon, inviting students from all over the East Coast to spend an amazing 24 hours at Smith College building products that matter. We've been committed to a 50:50 gender ratio since Day 1, and every year we inch closer to disrupting the gender gap in tech! At HackSmith, expect great food, sleepless nights, and too much fun!

FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24-hour event where awesome people get together to make cool apps, websites, games, gadgets, etc...

Can I attend?

HackSmith is open for everyone! Well, as long as you're 18 and/or a college student. We do prefer curious, innovative, and creative individuals though.

Where should I go?

The opening ceremony will be in the Carroll Room on the 2nd floor of the Campus Center.

I'm new to this...

This is a great place to learn. We will have mentors and workshops throughout HackSmith to help you learn something new.

What about travel reimbursement?

Limited travel reimbursement will be provided to students who are not from the five-college area. Please indicate that you'd like travel reimbursement on your application.

And teams?

If you're in need of teammates or ideas for a hack, we will help match you during a team formation session the day of the hackathon.

Can I sleep overnight?

Yes, there will be a few hosts on campus! We encourage all hackers to take care of themselves and sleeeeep.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, phone, chargers, ID, sleeping bags - if you would like - and most of all a great hacking spirit!

Will you have hardware?

Unfortunately this year, we don't access to MLH's hardware lab

Can I volunteer?

The more, the merrier! If you're a Smithie, give us a holler.

My question isn't here...

Email us at scs@smith.edu, or send us a Facebook message.
